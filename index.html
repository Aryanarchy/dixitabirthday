My Hope
She is my hope, though I am
not hers,
a whispered prayer the
heavens never heard.
She moves like dawn across
my weary sky,
and I a star fading just to let
her shine.
She's special, you ask? Ah,
what makes her so?
She's the calm in the middle
of every storm,
I've ever tried to survive.
She's the only reason I still
believe that light can find its
way,
through a broken soul like
mine.
Shes the end to my ache, the
light in my fall,
the reason I still dream at all.
My Iris the girl who stepped
through my night,
and painted my darkness
with gentle light.
So, God, keep her heart
forever aglow,
even if mine must break to
let her go.
Let me suffer in silence, if I
must dwell,
in a heavenless love, a
beautiful hell.
